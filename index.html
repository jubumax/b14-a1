Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about DevConf 2026? Find the answers right here.

Will the sessions be recorded and available later?

Yes, all keynotes and main sessions will be recorded. Registered attendees (Regular and VIP Pass holders) will get exclusive access to the video archive after the event ends.

How do I verify my Student Pass?

After purchasing a Student Pass, you will receive an email requesting a valid student ID check. Simply upload a copy of your ID, and your ticket will be verified within 24 hours.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer full refunds up to 7 days before the event starts. Send us a request via our support email, and we will process it right away.

Will I get a certificate of attendance?

Yes! All ticket holders (including Student Pass) will receive an official digital certificate of participation from DevConf 2026 via email.
